I am currently a Master's student (MASt) in Cambridge's Part III program studying mathematics with a concentration in theoretical physics. I am passionate about understanding emergence in condensed matter systems and numerical approaches for the quantum many-body problem.

I carried out my Bachelors degrees in Physics and Computer Science at the University of Berkeley, participating in research in both soft and hard condensed matter under Edgar Knobloch, Michael Zaletel, and Joel Moore.

I am in the process of uploading notes, materials, and code to this website to aid potential researchers in the field. Please feel free to contact me with any questions, suggestions, or to colloborate!
