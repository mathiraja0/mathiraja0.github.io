I am currently a Master's student (MASt) in Cambridge's Part III program studying mathematics with a concentration in theoretical physics. I am passionate about understanding emergence in condensed matter systems and numerical approaches for the quantum many-body problem. I previously studied at UC Berkeley where I had the opportunity to work on soft and hard condensed matter systems. In soft matter, I worked on a project regarding the nonlinear dynamics of localized solutions in a Swift-Hohenberg equation. In hard matter, I worked on various projects using tensor network methods to study ground states of lattice models, including fractional Chern insulators and extensions to the Hubbard model.

Please feel free to contact me with any questions, suggestions, or to colloborate!
